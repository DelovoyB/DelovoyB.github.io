BigWatchMaker presents new collection of Crude:

Crude is rough and yet delicate. Unique, but not elitist. Luxurious, but not showy. Worked perfectly and never finished. And rough enough to stand a little above things. Because when our mission started, some thought we were crazy. Others for naive or even ignorant: "It doesn't work that way, it doesn't work, nobody does it that way." But, we said, we do it that way. We relaxed, but stubbornly stuck to our vision: we wanted to create a precision watch from a cast silver. Against all contradictions and resistances.
