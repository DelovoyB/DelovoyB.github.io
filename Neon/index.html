Sky blue
Blue - the name of the group of colors. The spectral blue color is felt by a person under the influence of electromagnetic radiation with wavelengths in the range of 440–485 nm. One of the primary colors in the KZS system.
Grass green
Green is one of three, along with red and blue primary colors, green is the color of visible electromagnetic radiation with wavelengths lying in the range of approximately 510-550 nm. In the CMYK system in printing, green is obtained by mixing yellow and blue-green.
Sun yellow
Yellow - colors of electromagnetic radiation with wavelengths from 550 to 590 nm. It is complementary to blue in RGB or complementary to purple in art practice and the RYB system. However, in antiquity, due to the imperfection of the pigments available, it was considered as additional to purple.
Fire red
Red, also scarlet, bright - the region of flowers in the long-wavelength part of the visible spectrum corresponds to the minimum frequencies of electromagnetic radiation perceived by the human eye.
